Who I am

Past

My name is Edwar Fanoun. I was born in July 27, 2002 in Aleppo, Syria. Where I grew up and lived most of my life with my parents and little brother. Because of the war in Syria, we decided to come to Sweden for a better and safer future. We arrived in Sweden in October 2015.

Present

Currently, I'm a technology student at NTI Gymnasiet in Södertälje. I like NTI because I study subjects that I'm interested in like programming and game development. The teachers are very helpful and take very good care of the students. I like to play computer games with my friends, watch anime, and read comics and books in my free time.

Future

I'm uncertain about my future and which career path I'm going to take. But I know for sure that it will involve computers and a lot of programming. I will to continue study, finish highschool and go to university. After that we'll have to see what the future holds.
